About

Pre-Fuel is a web application which performs machine learning on datasets retreieved from NSW Government to predict future fuel prices. Prediction is made using Python's Scikit using the Regression Model.

Area

Enter an area, brand and type of fuel whose price you want to predict.

Predict

Get a prediction of fuel prices in your area for upto two weeks.

Refill

Refill you fuel tanks on the day which is the cheapest to make the most savings.
